◆ About

OrcAgent is a social trading platform for Solana meme coins. Connect a dedicated wallet, follow traders you trust, and let a rules-based bot execute your strategy automatically — or trade manually whenever you want full control.

How it works

  1. Connect a wallet. OrcAgent generates a separate, dedicated trading wallet for your bot and manual trades — this is not the same wallet you sign in with, and your seed phrase never leaves your own wallet app.
  2. Set your rules. Choose a breakout trigger, take profit, stop loss, and how many positions the bot can hold at once.
  3. The bot scans the market. It continuously checks live Solana tokens against your rules, runs basic safety checks (mint/freeze authority, rug-pull signals) before entering, and exits automatically when your take-profit or stop-loss is hit.
  4. Follow the social layer. See a live feed of trades and posts from traders you follow, message them directly, and optionally copy their trades at your own position size.

What you can do

  • Automate entries and exits with your own breakout / TP / SL rules
  • Trade manually in Live Market using the same live token data the bot sees
  • Follow other traders and copy their trades in real time
  • Track a live feed of trades and posts from people you follow
  • Chat directly with other traders, share images and trade cards
OrcAgent is a tool for automating your own trading rules. It is not investment advice, and no automated strategy removes the risk of loss.
▤ Docs

Getting started with the bot

  1. Connect a Phantom or Solflare wallet to sign in — use one that isn't holding your main savings.
  2. Fund your dedicated trading wallet (shown on the Wallet page) with SOL — this is what the bot actually trades with and pays network fees from.
  3. Under Bot Settings, set:
    • Breakout Trigger — the minimum price move that qualifies a token as an entry candidate.
    • Take Profit / Stop Loss — the % gain or loss at which the bot automatically closes a position.
    • Max Concurrent Positions — how many tokens the bot can hold open at the same time.
  4. Toggle Auto Trading on when you're ready. You can pause it at any time — open positions stay open and are still managed by your TP/SL rules while paused.

How the bot decides to trade

The bot scans the live market for tokens moving past your configured breakout threshold, checks mint/freeze authority and runs a rug-pull check before entering. It exits a position automatically once your take profit or stop loss is reached — no manual action needed.

Live Market

Live Market shows the same real-time, scored token list the bot scans — price change, volume, and risk signals for each token. Tap a token to open the quick trade panel and buy or sell manually, at any time, independent of whether Auto Trading is on. Use it to trade opportunities yourself or to see what the bot is currently watching.

Wallet

The Wallet page shows your dedicated trading wallet — a separate address from the one you connected with. Deposit SOL to its address to fund trading and network fees, and withdraw back to your own wallet at any time. Keeping trading funds in this dedicated wallet (rather than your main wallet) limits what the bot can ever touch.

Copy trading

Enable copy trading on a trader's profile to automatically mirror their entries at your own configured position size. Your own TP/SL rules still apply to copied positions — copy trading only mirrors entries, not their exit timing.

OrcAgent charges a {{ fee_pct }}% performance fee, taken only from profitable closed trades. It's calculated on the profit of that specific trade and sent as a small, separate SOL transaction from your trading wallet right after the position closes — it is not deducted from the sell proceeds themselves.

Losing trades and open (not-yet-closed) positions are never charged. If a trade closes at a loss or break-even, no fee is taken at all.

Network fees

Every on-chain trade also pays standard Solana network and swap-routing fees, set by the network and the swap route, not by OrcAgent. These apply on every trade regardless of whether it's profitable.

Fee rates are configurable and may change. The rate shown above is read live and is always the one currently in effect.
◈ Security

Your wallet & keys

OrcAgent never has access to your seed phrase — that stays in your own Phantom/Solflare wallet app. The bot instead trades through a separate, server-generated trading wallet. That trading wallet's private key is encrypted twice before it's ever stored: once with a server-held encryption key, and again with a second key derived from your own wallet address. Both layers are required to recover a usable key, so a database leak alone is not enough to expose it. The key is only decrypted momentarily, in memory, at the exact moment a trade is signed.

Session security

Wallet connections use Sign-In With Solana, with a fresh, server-issued, single-use nonce for every login — nothing about your session can be replayed. Optional Face ID / WebAuthn login is available for returning faster without re-signing every time.

Trade safety checks

Before entering a position, the bot validates a token's mint and freeze authority and runs a rug-pull risk check (liquidity, volume, and known scam patterns). These checks reduce exposure to obviously unsafe tokens, but no automated check can eliminate risk entirely — always trade with funds you can afford to lose.

What this doesn't protect against

Security here is about protecting your keys and session — it can't protect you from a token itself losing value, from market volatility, or from a signed transaction you approved yourself. Only fund your trading wallet with what you're willing to actively trade with.
